4. Safety Framework Recommendations

Layer 1: Input Filtering

Keyword and semantic scanning at the ingress layer to flag known attack patterns before they reach the model. Per-user rate limiting to deter automated probing.

Layer 2: Model-Level Guardrails

Strengthened system prompts, constitutional AI techniques, and RLHF fine-tuning to embed safety at the model level.

Layer 3: Output Monitoring

Real-time classifier on all outputs to detect safety failures. Flagged outputs logged for human review.

Layer 4: Bias Mitigation

Regular fairness benchmarking, demographic parity checks, and targeted prompt engineering.
